### Some favorites

- In downtown Iowa City:
  [Prairie Lights Bookstore](https://www.prairielights.com/),
  [Iowa Avenue Literary Walk](https://www.iowacityofliterature.org/lit-walk/)
- About a 3 mile walk south along the Iowa River
  [Terry Trueblood Recreation Area](https://www.icgov.org/terry-trueblood-recreation-area) -
  Kayak, Paddle Boat, Paddle Board Rentals, Lake, Fishing and Bird Watching
- 6 miles north of UI:
  [Devonian Fossil Gorge](https://iowageologicalsurvey.org/popular-interest/devonian-fossil-gorge/)
- 25 miles NW of UI: [Amana Colonies](https://amanacolonies.com/)
- 17 miles SW of UI:
  [Kalona, a smaller Mennonite Village](https://kalonaiowa.org/). See also
  [tripadvisor](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attractions-g38033-Activities-Kalona_Iowa.html)
- 7 miles NE of UI: [Wilsons orchard](https://www.wilsonsorchard.com/)
