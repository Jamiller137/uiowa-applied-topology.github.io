**May 16-20, 2022**  
**University of Iowa**

## Overview

The Topological Data Visualization Workshop was a 5-day event held at the
University of Iowa, combining tutorials and research talks on topological
techniques for visualizing data.

### Organizers

-   **[Isabel Darcy](https://homepage.math.uiowa.edu/~idarcy/)**, University of
    Iowa
-   **[Bei Wang](https://www.sci.utah.edu/~beiwang/)**, University of Utah

**Funding:** NSF DMS RTG 2038103 (PI:
[Keiko Kawamuro](https://kawamuro.weebly.com/))

**Location:**
[Seamans Center](https://www.facilities.uiowa.edu/named-building/seamans-center)
[Room 3630](https://classrooms.uiowa.edu/sc-3630)
